    <div class="policy-section">
        <h2>1. Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Prevention</h2>
        <p>Tulong App maintains a zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation. Our comprehensive prevention measures include:</p>
        
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Age Verification:</strong> All users must be 18 years or older to create accounts and participate in volunteer activities</li>
            <li><strong>Identity Verification:</strong> Volunteers undergo verification processes before being approved for tasks involving vulnerable populations</li>
            <li><strong>Supervised Interactions:</strong> All help requests and volunteer activities are logged and monitored</li>
            <li><strong>Content Monitoring:</strong> All communications, photos, and content are subject to automated and manual review</li>
            <li><strong>Background Screening:</strong> Volunteers working with families or in sensitive situations undergo additional screening</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="policy-section">
        <h2>2. Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) Prevention</h2>
        <p>Tulong App employs multiple layers of protection against CSAM:</p>
        
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Automated Detection:</strong> All uploaded images are scanned using industry-standard CSAM detection technology</li>
            <li><strong>Human Review:</strong> Flagged content undergoes immediate human review by trained moderators</li>
            <li><strong>Immediate Removal:</strong> Any suspected CSAM is immediately removed and reported to authorities</li>
            <li><strong>Account Suspension:</strong> Users uploading inappropriate content face immediate permanent suspension</li>
            <li><strong>Law Enforcement Cooperation:</strong> Full cooperation with law enforcement investigations</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="policy-section">
        <h2>3. In-App Reporting System</h2>
        <p>Tulong App provides multiple ways for users to report child safety concerns:</p>
        
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Report Button:</strong> Available on all user profiles and help requests</li>
            <li><strong>Emergency Contact:</strong> Direct access to child safety contact information</li>
            <li><strong>Anonymous Reporting:</strong> Users can report concerns without revealing their identity</li>
            <li><strong>24/7 Monitoring:</strong> Reports are reviewed immediately upon receipt</li>
            <li><strong>Follow-up Process:</strong> All reports receive acknowledgment and status updates</li>
        </ul>

        <div class="highlight">
            <strong>How to Report:</strong>
            <ol>
                <li>Tap the "Report" button on any user profile or content</li>
                <li>Select "Child Safety Concern" from the report categories</li>
                <li>Provide detailed information about the concern</li>
                <li>Submit the report - it will be reviewed within 1 hour</li>
            </ol>
        </div>
    </div>

            <li><strong>Mandatory Reporting:</strong> All suspected child abuse cases are reported to appropriate authorities within 24 hours</li>
            <li><strong>Data Preservation:</strong> Evidence is preserved and made available to law enforcement as required</li>
            <li><strong>Regular Audits:</strong> Quarterly reviews of child safety procedures and compliance</li>
        </ul>
